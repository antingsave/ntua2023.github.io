三宅将平Masahira Miyake

敬請期待

I work in the Japanese animation industry as a storyboarder and episode director. After graduating from Ritsumeikan University's Department of Image Arts and Sciences, joined Studio Bones. Currently working as a freelancer on David Production's films. Recently worked as an assistant director on Spriggan.

The power of the Japanese animation industry and the problems it faces

I will explain about the Japanese animation industry, its strengths and its flip side, its shortcomings. Then, as an example of one of the participants there, I will talk about how I set myself up in the animation industry, with what kind of prospect, and what kind of experience I am actually having and how I see the future.
